How to Use This Dashboard

1. Programs Tab — eight curated programs across three tiers. Each expandable card has the full breakdown: cost, length, modality, admissions, pros/cons, scholarships, and a Gabe Input section where you capture his reactions in real time.

2. Compare Tab — side-by-side table for quick comparison across all the dimensions that matter.

3. Decision Helper — shows his rated rankings, top picks, and a forced-choice exercise.

4. Application Tracker — once you've narrowed to 3–4 programs, manage deadlines, requirements, essay status, recommenders, and submission tracking here.

5. Timeline — visual roadmap of what needs to happen when for a Sept 2027 start.

Sample Timeline for September 2027 Start

Adjust based on the actual programs you target. If pursuing Jan 2027 start, compress this by 9 months.

Now — June 2026

Narrow target list to 3–5 programs

Use this dashboard, do informational interviews with sustainability consultants, attend 1–2 admissions webinars per program. Confirm GPA and decide on need for additional work experience.

June — August 2026

Deepen the PERC project narrative & build relationships

Continue Zips internship through summer. Document outcomes (any cost savings? customer response data? owner decisions?). Identify 2 recommenders: ideally 1 Penn State professor + 1 from Main Street Media or Zips owner. Have coffee with each, share goals, plant the ask.

September 2026

Draft Statement of Purpose v1

Anchor on the PERC project. Story arc: communications grad → applied sustainability project → recognition of consulting fit → why this program. Get Peri's review. Tailor 2–3 versions per program type (business-school programs vs applied science programs read differently).

October 2026

Hit Kogod priority deadline + Penn State early

American/Kogod's October 15 priority scholarship deadline. Penn State World Campus has rolling admissions but earlier is better. Formally ask recommenders, give them 4 weeks + your resume + draft SoP.

November — December 2026

Submit remaining applications

December 15 is Kogod's priority scholarship round 2. Most other programs have January–March priority deadlines. Submit by Dec 31 for best scholarship consideration broadly. Bard rolling.

January — March 2027

Interviews & decision waves

Most programs interview Jan–March. Decisions typically Feb–April. File FAFSA in January. Visit campuses (or attend virtual admitted student events) for top 2.

April — May 2027

Make final decision & deposit

Compare aid packages, negotiate scholarships if possible (Bard, Kogod, RIT all have history of matching competing offers), put down deposit. Notify other schools politely.

May — August 2027

Pre-enrollment prep

Complete any prerequisites (econ/accounting for Bard MBA, bridge courses for RIT if applicable). Housing logistics if relocating. Plan transition from Zips.

August — September 2027

Start program

Orientation, first courses. Connect with career office in week 1 — don't wait until year 2 to think about consulting recruitment.
